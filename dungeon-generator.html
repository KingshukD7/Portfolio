Algorithm

BSP Tree
Binary Space Partitioning

Recursively divides space into smaller sections, then places rooms within each partition. Creates structured, game-like dungeons with clear room separation. Used in games like Binding of Isaac.

Cellular Automata
Cave-like structures

Starts with random noise, then applies rules based on neighbor cells over multiple iterations. Creates organic, cave-like environments. Perfect for roguelikes and natural formations.

Drunkard's Walk
Organic pathways

Simulates random walker carving paths through the dungeon. Creates winding, interconnected passages with organic flow. Great for exploration-focused games.
